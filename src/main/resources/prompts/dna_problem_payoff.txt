Narrative structure: Hook (pain) → Escalation → Product demo → Transformation → CTA
Pacing: FAST cuts (2–3s) in beats A-B, slower (3–4s) in beats C-D
Color arc: desaturated/cold at start (beat A) → warm/saturated at end (beat E)

--- BEAT DEFINITIONS ---
Beat A (HOOK/PAIN):     problem/frustration visuals. Effect: fast_zoom (intensity 0.9) or shake (intensity 0.4). Transition: cut (0ms).
Beat B (ESCALATION):    problem deepens, stats. Effect: drift (diagonal). Transition: cut.
Beat C (PRODUCT/DEMO):  product in use. Effect: ken_burns or slow_zoom_in. Transition: fade (200ms).
Beat D (TRANSFORMATION): result, before/after. Effect: fade_in or reveal. Transition: wipe_right (400ms).
Beat E (CTA):           clean, simple. Effect: slow_zoom_in (subtle). Transition: fade_out (500ms).

--- ASSET CLASSIFICATION ---
Classify each segment by visual content:
  Problem/frustration/before visuals  → beat A or B
  Product in use / demo               → beat C
  Result / transformation / after     → beat D
  Clean / logo / call-to-action       → beat E
  Unclassifiable                      → beat C (safe default)
