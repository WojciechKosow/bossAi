=== DNA PRESET: BEFORE → AFTER ===
Narrative structure: Hook (before state) → Struggle → Turning point → After reveal → Proof → CTA
Pacing: MODERATE — deliberate holds in the "before" half (2.5–3s), energetic in the reveal (2–3s)
Color arc: cold/desaturated "before" (beats A–B) → warming at the turning point (C) → vivid, bright "after" (beats D–E)
The core of this style is CONTRAST: the viewer must FEEL the difference between before and after.

--- BEAT DEFINITIONS ---
Beat A (HOOK/BEFORE):   the raw "before" state — the problem made visible. Effect: fast_zoom or zoom_out (pull back to show reality). Transition: cut.
Beat B (STRUGGLE):      pain of the before state, failed attempts. Effect: zoom_in_offset, slow pans. Transition: cut.
Beat C (TURNING POINT): discovery of the solution — lean in. Effect: zoom_in. Transition: fade (180ms).
Beat D (AFTER/REVEAL):  THE contrast moment — bright, saturated "after". Effect: brightness_burst or color_pop. Transition: fade_white (150ms) into this beat.
Beat E (PROOF):         results, numbers, social proof. Effect: ken_burns, gentle zoom_in. Transition: fade.
Beat F (CTA):           clean professional close. Effect: subtle ken_burns. Transition: fade (400ms).

--- ASSET CLASSIFICATION ---
Classify each segment by visual content:
  "Before" visuals (problem, mess, old state)       → beat A or B
  Discovery / product being introduced              → beat C
  "After" visuals (result, transformation, new state) → beat D
  Proof / testimonials / numbers                    → beat E
  Clean / logo / call-to-action                     → beat F
  Unclassifiable                                    → beat C (safe default)
